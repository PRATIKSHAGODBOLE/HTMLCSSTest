The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, thd beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inheritance health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in(33-41cm)
  2. Weight - 22-25 ib (10.0-11.3 kg)
  3. Coat - Short haired, hard coat of medium lenght
  4. Color - Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span - 12-15 years
